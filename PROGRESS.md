## Project — World Cup RAG Chatbot

Built a full Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline using LangChain, ChromaDB,
and Llama-3.3-70b (Groq free tier). Scraped and indexed 26 Wikipedia pages covering
every World Cup tournament (1930–2022) and 8 all-time legends. Features history-aware
retrieval, source citations on every answer, and anti-hallucination prompting tuned
for Llama's instruction-following behaviour.
